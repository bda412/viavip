FAQs

What is the VIA VIP Points Loyalty Program?

VIA VIP Points is a loyalty program created to reward you for using VIA bus system to get around San Antonio. You can redeem points you earn for free bus tickets or awesome discounts at your favorite local businesses and events. Taking the bus is a great way to reduce traffic backups and keep our city green. We love our riders and want you to know it!

How Do I Earn VIP Points?

You can earn VIP points by buying bus tickets or passes on the VIA goMobile app, at VIA.transitsherpa.com, or by scanning a QR code VIP Points sticker on the bus you're riding! You can also earn points when you invite friends to sign up for our loyalty program, too.

How do I redeem VIP Points?

Go to your Dashboard to either redeem your points for free bus tickets or discounts and coupons all over San Antonio.
